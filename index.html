The Vision

The Bluebird project is bacically a project which teaches children how to do proper artwork. It can tell if they are meant to be little artists. The list below will tell you what things you will need:

  • Pencil,
  • Pen,
  • Paper,
  • Canvas,
  • Paint-brush,
  • Paints,
  • A steady hand
  • And of course a creative imagination!
